ABOUT CHARITY DONATIONS AND ALLOCATION

Charity Donations and Allocation is an Kenyan charity managed by a board of passionate individuals dedicated to improving the lives of people across Kenya. We aim to do this through availing shoes, clothes and financial assistance to where they are most needed and in time.

our mission
